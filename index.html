FAQ

How do I order a book?

You can place an order in two ways:

  • through the Amazon online store in your country.
  • through any bookstore in your region. In your internet search bar, select the desired book from our website, copy its title into the search bar, choose an online bookstore you like, and follow the instructions on their website to complete the purchase, filling in the required information for payment and delivery.
How quickly will my order arrive, and what is the delivery cost?

Delivery time and cost depend on the store you choose to order the book from and their terms.

You’ll need to check whether the book is in stock or printed on demand.

Do you offer international delivery?

We recommend purchasing the book from a store in your region. This will be more cost-effective for delivery. We do not sell books directly — we work with stores and platforms worldwide.

See the answer to “How do I order a book?” (active link).

How do I pay for my order?

We sell books through bookstores and distributor platforms. Payment methods depend on the chosen store and book format. See the answer to “How do I order a book?” (active link). Most stores offer several standard payment options, such as credit card, PayPal, or other available methods.

Where can I read book reviews?

You can read reviews on our website: cosm-unity.com.

How can I contact CosmUnity Publishing for collaboration inquiries?

For partnership or collaboration questions, please email us at the address listed in the Contact Us section on our website.

How can I leave a book review?

To leave a review, go to the online store where you purchased the book and post it there. Alternatively, you can email your review to us at info@cosm-unity.com — we’d be grateful!

Can I purchase books in bulk?

Yes, we offer wholesale purchases. Please contact us, and we’ll provide special terms: info@cosm-unity.com.

What should I do if my book arrived with a defect?

If your book has a defect, please contact the customer support service of the store or platform where you placed your order. We are not the direct seller of our books but are committed to their high quality. If you have difficulty with a return, please email us using the address in the Contact Us section and we’ll do our best to assist you.

15/05/2024
Ernst Timur Dien

As a nonfiction author, producer, and coach, I create texts, podcasts, and videos on current topics in the field of science and research (for example, for Stifterverband). During my years of study, I had the privilege of meeting some of the great thinkers in the humanities who give courage, knowledge, and guidance—especially in difficult times. I think, for example, of Tolstoy, Heraclitus, Lessing, Schelling, Rilke, and Goethe, as well as the books of Muhammad Asad, a courageous reformer of Islam in the early 20th century, or the political views of Pepe Mujica, former President of Uruguay and one of the great humanists of our time.

What once might have seemed strange can, after several decades, turn out to be fundamental—and even necessary. That is why in our time there is a renewed interest in the works of Baruch Spinoza, Adalbert Stifter, Nicholas of Cusa, Jakob Böhme, and Blaise Pascal.

Why am I writing this to you? I would like to recommend two Russian authors whose works may at first seem unconventional.

Rarely has a new system of thought and knowledge awakened me and led me to a new way of thinking, feeling, and being as much as reading and understanding the books of L. Seklitova and L. Strelnikova. In the Russian cultural sphere, there have often emerged new impulses that seemed mysterious to contemporaries, whose deeper meaning could only be revealed through careful study. This was exactly my experience when I became acquainted with the works of these two authors.

I kindly ask you to consider adding the most significant works of L. Seklitova and L. Strelnikova—which have already been translated—to your collection.

First of all, thank you for taking the time to read this letter. May you also find the time to give a new work the chance to reveal itself—to overcome the first possible obstacle in the form of provocation during the initial moments of reading. Perhaps it will prove valuable for you.

With best wishes, Ernst Timur Dien https://www.bewusstseinsberatung.net/buecher-videos/
